What is a MUN ?

A MUN (or Model of United Nations) is a scholar simulation where participants have to act as delegates of the United Nations. The goal of these simulations is to teach students how the United Nations works as well as international diplomacy In a MUN, the participants represent a country and are associated to a specific comity (as the security council or UNICEF). They debate about worldwide issues, negociate with others, and write what is called resolutions to solve the main issue (as mentionned right below). They of course have to keep the stance of their assigned country.
