Conferences

Negociation sessions are split up into two committees : One in french and one in english. Both of them take place simultaneously in two different rooms with two different topics. This offers a unique experience for everyone no matter their proficiency in English.

Conference in French

During the 2024 session of CHAUMUN, the French committee was about sustainable development and took place in a room at the town hall. A particularly well-decorated room that added a solemn dimension to the debates. Here are some photos from CHAUMUN 2024:

Conference in English

At the same time, the UNESCO conference in English took place with students from Czechia, Poland, Azerbaijan, Germany, and Taiwan. It was held a room at the Canopé in Chaumont and focused on access to education for girls in the world.
