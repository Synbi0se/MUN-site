What is a MUN?

A MUN, or Model United Nations, is an educational simulation where participants play the role of United Nations delegates. These simulations aim to teach students about the workings of the United Nations and international diplomacy. In a MUN, participants represent a country and are assigned to a specific committee (such as the Security Council or UNICEF). Their task is to debate global issues, negotiate with other delegates, and draft resolutions to solve these issues, while adhering to the official position of their assigned country.
